Bioengineering

I am in the computational Biology concentration of bioengineering, and am specifically interested in protein interaction modeling. Previously I have worked in the Teresa Head-Gordon Lab applying the Poisson-Boltzmann Semi-Analytical Method onto polymer models.

Software Engineering

Software is an amazing tool that allows us to solve bigger and harder problems. It lets us find the shortest path to our next class, sort our busy schedules and stream the next big meme. But I think software engineering can be used for even bigger and better things, like modeling protein folding or connecting the world or even getting us to Mars. And I want to be a part of this process.
